Creating Entrepreneurs at The Ohio State University

CoStart is more than an organization; it is a movement designed to form dynamic connections and provide the resources needed to build an ecosystem capable to fostering an idea from inception to implementation. We aim to bring Ohio State Students together in order to coach entrepreneurship skills, provide a valuable network or resources and personnel, discuss potential inventions and ideas, and foster the refinement and development of those ideas.
